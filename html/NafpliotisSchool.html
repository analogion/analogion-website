Η σχολή του Ιάκωβου Ναυπλιώτη / The Old Patriarchal School
Serious, majestic, ecclesiastical chanting
About the school
Serious, majestic, ecclesiastical chanting. Emphasis on chronos and rhythm. Good intervals. More emphasis on oral tradition than written tradition.
