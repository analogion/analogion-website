  • Κώδικες της Ορθοδόξου Βυζαντινής Εκκλησιαστικής Ασματωδίας, Άγγελος Βουδούρης/ Kodikes tes Orthodoxou Byzantines Ecclesiastikes Asmatodias, Angelos Boudouris

    This is the set of Boudouris' original manuscripts photocopied into books. The 18 volumes contain:

    1. Oktoechos A
    2. Oktoechos B
    3. Anastasimatarion A
    4. Anastasimatarion B
    5. Heirmologion
    6. Menologion
    7. Dodekaemeron
    8. Triodion A
    9. Triodion B
    10. Megale Evdomas
    11. Pentecostarion A
    12. Pentecostarion B
    13. Typikon
    14. Mousika Mathemata (selected pieces from around the year)
    15. Eisagoge eis ten Byzantinen Mousiken (method/theoretikon)
    16. Mousika Syggramata kai Meletes (catalogues of musical manuscripts)
    17. Mousikokritika (various musicological and historical topics)
    18. Mousikologika Apomnemoneumata (His and Nafpliotis' psaltic career and other historical facts), Contains his article in the Orthodoxia Magazine and his Hypomnema to the Patriarch (both available on the Analogion).

    Published by his grandson Angelos L. Boudouris and the European Art Centre, Athens, Greece. Available through his grandson (email us for his phone number) for ~300 euros (for the set). Back in 2005 there were a few sets available. The collection of books is made available on-line at avoudouris.com. Many scanned and translated samples of the above works can be found on the Analogion [html ].
