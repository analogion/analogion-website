Ψαλτικό Υλικό στα Σλαβονικά / Slavonic Psaltic Resources
Συλλογή Σλαβονικών ψαλτικών βιβλίων / A collection of Slavonic books
Εισαγωγή / Introduction
The books presented below as well as the original commentary were scanned and offered by Dusan Radosavljevic (and his sources for some of the books) whom we thank on behalf of all the people that will benefit from this material.
Βιβλία / Books
Here is a list of books with a short indicative content summary, ordered by author. For more details, most of the files have a table of contents listed in the back.
  • Ilia Atanasov
    • Strasna Sedmica I Pashata, Ilia Atanasov, 1941
    Book I. is Holy Week and Pascha.
    Book II is an anthology of material for Vespers, Matins & Divine Liturgy.
    [pdf, 48 Mb]
  • T. Ikonomov
    • Pesnopenie, T. Ikonomov, 1872
    Contains a brief pedagogic introduction, abbreviated Anastasimatarion, and materials for Vespers, Matins & Divine Liturgy.
    [pdf, 28 Mb]
  • Ivanov
    • Angel Irmologia, Ivanov, 1875
    Irmologion.
    [pdf, 8 Mb]
  • Zh. Pavlov
    • Triod i Pentikostar, Zh. Pavlov, 1907
    Triodion and Pentikostarion.
    [pdf, 34 Mb]
  • Petar Sarafov
    • Rukovodstvo, Petar Sarafov, 1912
    Extensive theoretical/pedagogic material, a short biography of Koukouzelis, and then a selection of what he calls "Parachordal" or "Super-modulating" material, the Anastasimatarion, materials from Matins, Divine Liturgy & Kalophonic selections.
    [pdf, 86 Mb]
  • Manasia Pop Teodorov
    • Psaltikiina Liturgia, Manasia Pop Teodorov, 1905
    Divine Liturgy. A part of a Pandekte-like series, each beginning with "Psaltic-". Published, I believe, originally in a single tome.
    [pdf, 12 Mb]
    Psaltikiini Trebi, Manasia Pop Teodorov, 1911
    Material for rites and services.
    [pdf, 5.7 Mb]
    Psaltikien Voskresnik, Manasia Pop Teodorov, 1914
    Anastasimatarion.
    [pdf, 9 Mb]
    Psaltikien Mineinik, Manasia Pop Teodorov, 1992 (reprint)
    Festal Idiomela. Similar to Kypseli Vol. I.
    [pdf, 57 Mb]
    Psaltikien Triod, Manasia Pop Teodorov, 1922 (reprint)
    Triodion & Pentikostarion material. Similar to Kypseli Vol. II.
    [pdf, 76 Mb]
    Psaltikina Utrena, Manasia Pop Teodorov, 1992 (reprint)
    Matins.
    [pdf, 40 Mb]
  • Nikolai Triandafilov
    • Cvetosobranie Liturgia, Nikolai Triandafilov, 1847
    Divine Liturgy material.
    [pdf, 104 Mb]
    Cvetosobranie Vecernaja i Utrennaja, Nikolai Triandafilov, 1847
    Vespers and Matins material.
    [pdf, 5 Mb]
    Voskresnik, Nikolai Triandafilov, 1847
    Anastasimatarion.
    [pdf, 80 Mb]
