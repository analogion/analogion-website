Are they really Third mode?
Why is the diatonic fthora of NH placed on the basis GA of such compositions?
Papadic pieces of Third mode are notated with the diatonic fthora of NH on GA and theory books say that they "progress as in Plagal Fourth mode". However, when we hear them chanted by traditional psaltai they sound like Third mode. Why then put the fthora of NH there?
