Comments

Comments by G.K. Michalakis on his interpretations
1990 Live = non traditional version, personal patchwork, trying to "make the meaning come out clear" as quickly as possible. Recordings are provided so as to prove that "experimentation" was done... conclusion: traditional Iakovos = the BEST...

2006 version: traditional version and tempo, approximate version of what was chanted in the Patriarcheion. Notice that, even though the canonarchisma is "faltso" because of vocal instability, the words are "cut up" according to meaning and according to melody, and not just according to "comas" and other punctuation marks found in present day books...
