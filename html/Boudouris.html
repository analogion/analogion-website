Articles
Orthodoxia Magazine: Boudouris' famous article in Orthodoxia Magazine of the EP back in 1937. An insider's description of the background, functioning and responsibilities of the musical choirs of the Patriarchate. [pdf,7 Mb]

Ypomnema to the Patriarch: Angelos Boudouris wrote this to the Patriarch Fotios in 1931 as a desperate plea to save Byzantine Music as it had been preserved in the Ecumenical Patriarchate till then. He describes what needs to be done to save Byzantine Music tradition. The most important thing, after oral transmission of tradition is to scientifically systematise the theory and study of the music. [pdf,3.7 Mb]
