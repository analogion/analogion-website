Michail Lagoudakis offered this recording from a recent concert at Duke University. The 7 chanters performing come from  various backgrounds (different schools, including patriarchal and Karas) and the hymn was performed on the spot (without music) with audience participation  (clearly heard). This recording perhaps represents a "popular version" of the hymn.
