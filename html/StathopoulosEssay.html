This is the electronic version of a book published recently by the
Holy Metropolis of Ilia, Greece, authored by chanter and teacher Mr
George Stahopoulos. George is one of the main Byzantine Music teachers
of the school of the Metropolis which as a matter of fact has around
150 (!) students - most of them young kids. The school's artistic
director is L. Angelopoulos. The students are taught not only
Byzantine chant but also traditional instruments and folk music.

The above essay is the source that K. Zarakovitis criticised recently
on the cmkon.org site (http://www.cmkon.org/Arthrografia.htm)
