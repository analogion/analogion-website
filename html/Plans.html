(English)
Future plans of analogion.com include (in no particular order):
The extension of the historical recordings archive as well as the collection of music scores and musicological articles.
The analytical presentation and commenting of existing material already presented on the site.
The addition of an extended, in-depth, theoretical/practical presentation of Psaltiki using references, recordings, scores and videos to prove the content according to the views of the different Psaltic schools of thought.
The translation of the site into both Greek and English.
The presentation and (constructive) criticism of more people who work for the benefit of the Psaltic Art, many of whom are relatively unknown, especialy outside their own country.
The completion of presentation and (constructive) criticism of the work of Simon Karas.
The enrichment of the electronic library with scores and texts useful to the Psaltic community, however, many of them still inaccessible to most psaltai like the manuscript transcriptions of Chourmouzios Chartophylax and others.
The promotion and support of old Patriarchal and Athonite tradition as the authentic reference basis for learning the art and the study of its most genuine representatives.
