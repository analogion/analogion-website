Ήδη Βάπτεται Κάλαμος / Idi Vaptetai Kalamos
Δοξαστικό Ιδιόμελο Αποστίχων Μεγ. Παρασκευής / Idiomelon of Great Friday
This famous composition by Iakovos Protopsaltis is usually chanted at Kai nyn of the Aposticha of the Orthros of Holy and Great Friday, Plagal Fourth Mode. Some psaltai chant a condensed melody of the Iakovos' original.
