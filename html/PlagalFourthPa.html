Pa of the FourthPlagal Mode
Raised on ascent, when and how
Theory
The Music Committee of 1881 provides an unclear description [jpg]. According to it Pa of the Fourth Plagal Mode most of the time israised by one tmema (two moria), as Ke of the Fourth Mode. When there exists melodic attraction towards Bou, Pa is raised by two tmemata (four moria) .

Related makam
Makam Rast has been identified as an equivalent of the Fourth Plagal Mode since long time, the earliest reference being Panagiotis Chalatzoglou's treatise on external music. The same is repeated in Konstantinos Protopsaltis and Stephanos Domestikos' "Interpretation of the External Music" (see p. 46).
